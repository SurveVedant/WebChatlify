Frequently Asked Questions
Is my identity safe on WebChatlify?
Yes, WebChatlify ensures complete anonymity for all users. Your identity will not be revealed to anyone.
Are there any restrictions on the topics for chatting?
WebChatlify encourages respectful and positive conversations. However, there are no restrictions on any topics because we believe in free speech.
How can I start chatting on WebChatlify?
Simply click on the 'Chat Now' button on the homepage to begin anonymous chatting with other users.
Is there a limit on the number of chats I can have?
There is no limit on the number of chats you can have on WebChatlify. Feel free to engage in multiple conversations at once.
